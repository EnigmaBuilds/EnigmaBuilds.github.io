About Us

Enigma Builds was made out of Lethioti and WOLLAND's passion about building and terraforming. Since then, but even before that they've tried to make quality builds for affordable prices. They do their best to make sure that the end result is what you like and exactly what you want. That way anyone can watch their dream build come true and enjoy their experience while playing or running their server. We believe that a great server needs to have maps that reflect the time and effort that have been put into the release and that is something our builds can showcase for your server.
